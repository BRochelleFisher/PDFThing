AUXILIARY LIGHT

The following instruction is a general recommendation. The illustrations in this section may vary slightly from your application. Follow all applicable laws in your area.

  • Located the auxiliary light(1) located between the vertical supports

    of the oil cooler guard(2).

  • Loosen the hardware securing the auxiliary light(1) to the oil cooler guard(2) vertical supports.

  • With power on to the auxiliary light(1), adjust the spot light center(3)

    to be within a range of 7-10yds (6-9m)(4) from the front of

    the tractor.

  • Tighten the hardware securing the auxiliary light(1) to the oil cooler guard(2) vertical supports.
